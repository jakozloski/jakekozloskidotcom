Things I'd Like to See Exist

VC Deck Feedback Marketplace

A platform where startups pay VCs directly for honest, actionable feedback on pitch decks. Most VCs are afraid to give good feedback that could save founders months or years of time.

Human-Level Animal Intelligence

Elevating another animal species to human-level intelligence to explore what emerges and understand broader implications for consciousness.

Anduril for Civil Government

Software that makes non-military government agencies efficient and modern, like Anduril did for defense.

Companies Adopt Public Spaces

Enabling companies to sponsor NYC subway stations and other public spaces. Businesses handle beautification, maintenance, and improvements in exchange for prominent physical advertising and naming rights.

Pre-Meeting Personality & IQ Reports

Automatically send out Big Five personality and IQ scores of meeting participants ahead of time.

LLMs for Public Opinion

Using large language models to quickly and accurately capture public sentiment, providing actionable insights for policymakers.

Sushi Robots

Robotics to significantly reduce sushi labor costs, which are typically 30-50% of the total cost, significantly higher than other foods.

Nova Muse - NYC Museum of the Beautiful

An art museum in New York City exclusively showcasing contemporary art created explicitly to elevate the human spirit, affirm beauty, and champion excellence.

Founder & Investor Compounds

Exclusive residential compounds for founders and investors offering coworking stays with curated guest lists. Facilities include gyms, healthy food options, and an environment optimized for deep networking and collaboration.

Apartment OS

An integrated hardware-software OS specifically tailored for apartment living and management. Think of Nest but even more intuitive and vertically integrated with the entire residential stack.
